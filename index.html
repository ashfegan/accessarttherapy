Online Group Therapy 

Wedensdays 9.15 - 10.45am (GMT)

This is a group analytic, therapy group for adults. It will run for 90 minutes, weekly during term time and for a minimum of two years. Hosted online from Newark on Trent in the UK, this group is for people who are living locally and globally. There will be a maximum of eight, regular group members. The cost is Â£100 per month; payable monthly in advance.

The aim is to help people feel less isolated. This group offers space to be with others and make sense of personal, social and cultural issues. The group may be particularly useful to people who are feeling disconnected, worried, sad, angry or curious.

Why group therapy? Group work is essentially about human connection, activism, empowerment, meaning making, development and authentic social change. In online group therapy, being with others from around the world can give perspective and help us feel more in touch with reality. 

 







I am an Irish artist, community-based art psychotherapist and group therapist.
